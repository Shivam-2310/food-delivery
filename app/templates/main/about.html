ABOUT JUSTEAT

OUR STORY

JustEat was founded with a simple mission: to revolutionize the food ordering experience by making it quick, convenient, and enjoyable for everyone.

We believe that ordering food should be a seamless process that connects hungry customers with amazing restaurants, allowing them to discover new cuisines and enjoy their favorite meals without hassle.

Since our inception, we've been committed to building a platform that prioritizes user experience, empowering both customers and restaurant owners with intuitive tools and features.

OUR MISSION

"To transform food ordering into a delightful experience by connecting people with the food they love, supporting local restaurants, and delivering exceptional service every step of the way."

OUR VALUES

CUSTOMER FOCUS

We put our customers at the center of everything we do, continuously improving our platform based on their feedback and needs.

QUALITY

We're committed to excellence in every aspect of our service, from the restaurants we partner with to the technology we build.

PARTNERSHIP

We believe in building strong relationships with restaurants, empowering them with tools to grow their business and reach new customers.
